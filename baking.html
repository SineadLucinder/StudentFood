Shortbread

An English Classic, it couldn't miss in our list and with just 3 ingredients, you get amazing beked goods with little shopping AND little effort.
What you need:

  • 125g Butter
  • 55g caster sugar
  • 180g plain flour

What to do:
  1. Preheat oven to 190Â°C
  2. Beat butter and sugar together until smooth and then add the flour. Mix well.
  3. Roll out the dough until it is about 1cm thick and cu them into any shape you want - get creative!
  4. Put on a baking tray, sprinkle a little sugar on top and in the fridge it goes (for about 20 minutes)
  5. Now, you can put the tray in the oven and the shortbread will be ready in 15-20 minutes.
