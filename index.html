Home

Hi, and welcome to Student Food - a guide to cooking for students at Durham University! This website has been created as our project for CFG:17/18.


We created this website with the aim to make food easy for students!
Check out our student on a budget page if you're wanting recipes that are cheap and easy.
We also have vegetarian and german cooking if you're in the mood for something different.
If you're looking for some Christmas cheer, check out our baking page.
Alternatively, if you can't be bothered to cook - have a look at our restaurants page!

We are based in Durham!
